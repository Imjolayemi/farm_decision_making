# FarmWise AI
**Business Proposal: Revolutionizing Agriculture Through Accessible Artificial Intelligence**

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Agriculture is the backbone of the African economy, yet it struggles with severe inefficiencies. Every year, hard-working smallholder farmers lose up to 40% of their harvests due to preventable diseases, poor crop selection, and lack of market pricing transparency. 

**FarmWise AI** is a digital solution built to fix this. We have developed a simple, highly accessible smartphone application that puts an expert agronomist, a financial advisor, and a community of farmers directly into the hands of the people who need it most. By using smart technology (Artificial Intelligence), FarmWise AI helps farmers stop guessing and start growing profitably.

---

## 2. The Problem We Are Solving

Despite incredibly hard work, farmers face three massive wealth traps:
1. **Blind Planting:** Farmers often plant whatever seeds they have, without knowing if their specific soil will actually support it. This leads to stunted growth and wasted fertilizer.
2. **Preventable Crop Disease:** When a leaf turns yellow, a farmer often doesn't know how to treat it until the disease has already destroyed the whole farm.
3. **Being Cheated on Price:** Farmers are isolated. When middlemen arrive with trucks, they buy crops at unfairly low prices because the farmer doesn't know what the crop is actually selling for in big cities.

---

## 3. Our Solution: FarmWise AI

FarmWise AI solves these problems through an easy-to-use app that functions both online and completely offline (for rural areas). 

### How it Works:
- **Smart Crop Matchmaker:** The farmer enters basic soil info, and the app instantly tells them exactly which crop will grow best and make the most money.
- **The "Plant Doctor" Camera:** If a plant looks sick, the farmer simply takes a photo with their phone camera. Our AI instantly identifies the exact disease and tells the farmer what affordable local medicine to buy to cure it.
- **Live Market Prices & Forecasts:** The app shows the exact current prices of crops in major cities (like Lagos and Kano), and uses financial forecasting to tell the farmer if prices are about to go up or down. This eliminates the middleman advantage.
- **Micro-Loans Made Easy:** Unbanked farmers struggle to get loans. FarmWise AI watches how active a farmer is on the app (e.g., checking weather, posting questions) and automatically generates a "Farm Credit Score" that local partner banks can trust to issue micro-loans.

## 4. Why This Will Succeed (The Market Opportunity)

Food demand is skyrocketing, but food production is not keeping up. Governments and NGOs invest billions into agriculture, yet the tools never make it down to the actual local farmer. 

FarmWise is unique because:
- **It is highly scalable:** It costs us almost nothing to add a new farmer to the platform.
- **It works for everyone:** Whether on a modern smartphone or an old button-phone via text message (USSD), farmers can get advice.
- **Subscription Revenue Model:** The app is free to start, but for just ₦2,500 a month (less than the cost of one bag of fertilizer), commercial farmers get unlimited plant disease scans and live price forecasting tools. It pays for itself the first time they use it to save a crop or negotiate a better price.
